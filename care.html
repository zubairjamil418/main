About Us

Dedicated to Island Children Since 1999

We are a registered charity committed to providing high-quality childcare, educational programmes, and family support services across Jersey and Guernsey.

Our Mission & Vision

Island Children was founded with a simple belief: every child deserves access to quality care and education regardless of their circumstances. Over the past 25 years, we have grown from a small nursery to a comprehensive children's charity serving hundreds of families.

Our team of dedicated professionals works tirelessly to create safe, nurturing environments where children can learn, play, and develop to their full potential.

Our Nursery

Early Years Education

Our nurseries provide a stimulating, safe environment where children aged 0-5 can explore, learn, and grow through play-based education.

0-2 Years

Baby Room

A calm, nurturing space designed specifically for our youngest children with sensory play and individual care routines.

  • 1:3 staff ratio
  • Sensory development
  • Sleep room available
  • Daily parent updates
2-3 Years

Toddler Room

Active learning through exploration with structured activities that develop language, motor skills, and social confidence.

  • 1:4 staff ratio
  • Potty training support
  • Outdoor play daily
  • Creative arts & crafts
3-5 Years

Preschool

Preparing children for school with structured learning, phonics, mathematics, and independence skills in a fun environment.

  • 1:8 staff ratio
  • School readiness programme
  • Weekly forest school
  • Graduation ceremony

Why Your Support Matters

With your help, we can continue providing exceptional care and opportunities for children who need it most. Your donation funds educational materials, nutritious meals, specialist equipment, and subsidised places for vulnerable families.

  • £10 provides art supplies for a week
  • £25 funds a child's swimming lessons

  • £100 buys specialist sensory equipment
  • £250 funds our outreach programme
