02 / ABOUT US

Hardware that powers the cloud.

Rackaro was founded on a simple premise: data centres deserve better access to high-quality IT hardware without the enterprise markup. We bridge the gap between manufacturers and infrastructure operators.

With over a decade in the industry, we've built a global supply network that sources, refurbishes, and delivers everything from single servers to full data centre builds. Our clients range from hyperscale operators to colocation providers and enterprise IT teams.

Sustainability isn't a buzzword for us — it's built into our model. By extending the lifecycle of enterprise hardware, we help reduce e-waste while delivering cost savings of up to 70% compared to new equipment.
