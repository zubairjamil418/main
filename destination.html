Welcome to Your Island Home

Nestled on the rugged west coast of the Isle of Islay, our self-catering holiday home offers a genuine escape from the everyday. Wake to the sound of waves, spend your days exploring coastal paths and world-famous distilleries, and return to warmth and comfort as the sun sets over the Atlantic.

Whether you are here for the whisky, the wildlife, the walking, or simply the peace, this is a place to slow down and breathe deeply.

Discover Islay

World-famous whisky, dramatic coastlines, and incredible wildlife — all on your doorstep.

Whisky Tourism

The Whisky Coast

Islay is home to nine world-renowned distilleries including Laphroaig, Lagavulin, and Ardbeg. Tour the maltings, taste the peat, and take a bottle home.

Walking

Coastal Paths & Walks

From gentle beach strolls to challenging cliff-top hikes, Islay's coastline offers walks for every ability. The famous Mull of Oa provides dramatic sea views.

Wildlife

Birds & Wildlife

Spot white-tailed sea eagles, golden eagles, choughs, and thousands of wintering geese. Seals, otters, and even dolphins can be seen along the coast.
