Over 35 Years of Excellence

Leyland Decorative Mouldings is a family-run business specializing in high-quality decorative mouldings. We combine traditional craftsmanship with modern lightweight polyurethane technology to create beautiful, durable architectural details.

From period restoration projects to modern new builds, our extensive range of cornice, coving, ceiling roses and dado rails add character and value to any property.
